About Me

I am a PhD student and doctoral researcher at the UKRI AI Centre for Doctoral Training in Biomedical Innovation, based within the School of Informatics at the University of Edinburgh.

My core scientific work is grounded in hard mathematical algorithms, specifically within high-dimensional geometry, convex polytopes, and randomised geometric sampling. I apply these theoretical foundations to analyze structural controllability and the topological properties of gene regulatory networks.

I am currently co-supervised by Dr. Filippo Menolascina and Dr. Vissarion Fisikopoulos.
