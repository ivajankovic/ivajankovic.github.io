Research & Software

Methodologies and algorithmic architectures

Core Methodologies

Systems Biology & Structural Control

Analyzing biological pathway interactions using monotone dynamical system behaviors and structural controllability bounds. We map multi-tier interaction dynamics within complex Genome-Scale Metabolic Models (GSMMs) and Gene Regulatory Networks (GRNs) to predict regulatory outcomes.

High-Dimensional Geometry & Sampling

Developing scalable randomized algorithms designed for boundary and interior sampling configurations across complex, constrained convex polytopes. This supports efficient geometric calculations within metabolic flux cones.
